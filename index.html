About

The complete story of SonderAI!

  • December 2023

    The dawn of SonderAI!

    On the 19th of December of 2023, the world changed forever. Pablo Rubio, 15 years old at the time, with the goal of learning more about AI and business while helping others at the same time, founded an AI agency called SonderAI. The rest is history...

  • What is SonderAI?

    SonderAI is an AI agency, that helps small and medium businesses integrate AI into their processes and workflows to automate tasks and reduce costs. We work hard to personalise our solutions and services to the specific goals of our clients, since we feel each client is unique and special.

  • Why should you use AI?

    AI is the fastest-growing industry at the moment, and things are changing rapidly and constantly. At SonderAI, our goal is to help you take advantage of these new technologies before the competition, since AI is here to stay and everyone needs to take part in it, before it's too late!

  • Goals and Ambitions...

    In the future, we hope to continue learning alongside our clients, while doing everything we can to help them out. Eventually, we wish to become a leading contender at the forefront of the AI industry, advancing AI to create a safe and prosperous future!
